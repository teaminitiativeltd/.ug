Who we are!

Team Initiatives Limited (TIL) is a multi-disciplinary development consultancy firm, legally registered in Uganda since 27th February 2008 under the companies Act CAP.110. We are located in Kampala City at Teacher’s House, Plot 28/30, Second Floor, Suite 206, Bombo Road.

Our goal

We aim to deliver consultancy services that meet the expectations of our clientele. We commit to achieve this through:

Objectives

  • a careful identification and deployment of the most suitable technical staff that match the technical tasks we are expected to perform for our clientele, always based on their expressed needs.
  • ensuring that we undertake adequate preparation in consultation with our clients in order to produce results in the most possible and desirable time without curtailing our clients’ work-plans
  • ensure that we stick to the prescribed contractual terms that are finally determined and agreed during the inception phases of our work
